timer
=====

This was the hardest bit so far. The timing is about total CPU time consumed
  by any hive instance since the genesis of the culture.

That's CLOCK_PROCESS_CPUTIME_ID but lots of stuff doesn't work with that
  clock including pthread_cond_timedwait which would otherwise by exactly
  what I need. 

The bodge is to use linux's peculiar ability to send a signal to a 
  particular thread, do that from a timer that always exists (and 
  is usually armed) and re-arm the timer from either the killer 
  thread after killing stuff or a worker thread that makes a payment 
  causing meap[0] to become earlier.
